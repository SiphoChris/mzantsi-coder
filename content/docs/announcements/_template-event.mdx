# Submit Your Portfolio for Reaction

**Status:** 🟢 Open
**Deadline:** 30 May 2025
**Episode:** _(link when published)_

---

## What is this?

On the channel I'll be doing a live reaction and feedback session on viewer portfolio sites.
This is a chance to get honest, constructive feedback on your work in front of the community.

You don't need to have a polished, finished portfolio. Work-in-progress is welcome —
sometimes feedback at that stage is more useful.

## Who can submit?

Anyone. You don't need to be South African, you don't need to have been watching the channel
for a long time. If you have something built and you want feedback, submit it.

## What to submit

- A live URL (GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify — anything publicly accessible)
- A short note on what you'd like feedback on (optional but helpful)

## How to submit

Use the form below. Submissions close on the deadline above.

[**Submit your portfolio ↗**](https://forms.google.com/your-form-link-here)

---

## What happens after you submit?

Submissions will be reviewed and selected ones will be featured in the reaction episode.
Everyone who submits will get a response even if not featured.
