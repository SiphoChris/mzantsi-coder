# Welcome to Mzantsi Coder

This is the community hub for the [Mzantsi Coder](https://youtube.com/@mzantsicoder) YouTube channel.

Everything here lives beyond any individual video. You don't need to have watched anything to find what you're looking for — browse the sections, join an event, or pick up a challenge.

## The channel

Mzantsi Coder is about making coding simple, practical, relatable, and proudly South African.
Episodes follow a **What · Why · How** structure, and most courses come in multiple editions
so you can choose the pace that works for you.
